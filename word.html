To the "Mailings" tab on the ribbon!
You'll need two things: the document and the data source. Press "Select Recipients" and select your file.
Next, select "Start a Mail Merge," and select the type of document you need. Depending on what you need, you'll get a few options that we'll go over briefly.
  • Letter
  • Email
  • Labels or Name Tags
Letter
Easy. Select "Address Block" or "Greeting Line" to insert a field! Then, "Finish and Merge."
Email
The width of the document might change - no stress. Just type your email. Include a greeting line, or insert an individual merge field where you'd like in the body of your text, like below. Then, "Finish and Merge."
Labels or Name Tags
Select your formatting and add to a new document. Then, insert your fields. Remember to use the "Rules > Next Record" Command! Then, "Finish and Merge."

Creating Restricted and Fillable Forms
Applications:
  • Stop others from editing rules or formatting
  • Ensure alignment across multiple documents
  • Control authorship
First, the Developer Tab.
Restricting Changes
You're able to select what type of edits people can make: tracked changes, comments, or fill in forms.
To make changes, click on "Restrict Edits > Stop Protection"
Fillable Forms
Insert content controls for text, pictures, dates, checkboxes, and lists. Use the "Design Mode" to make edits.
Reviewing Documents
Enable track changes with simple markup and accepting and rejecting. Use comments to share thoughts. Compare and combine documents across versions. Try a tutorial!
Styles
Styles enable responsive formatting, tables of contents, outlines, and accessibility.
